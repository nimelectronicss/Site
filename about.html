📖 My Story

Hi! I'm Nima, an Electronics Engineering student with a deep passion for both hardware and software. Ever since I can remember, I've been fascinated by how the flow of electrons can bring a device to life and make it work.

For the past few years, I've been diving deep into Embedded Systems and C programming. I find it incredibly exciting how the line between hardware and software is getting blurrier every day — and I love that challenge.

My goal is to build products that are not only technically strong but also deliver a great user experience. That's why I decided to create an online portfolio — to showcase my work to the world.

🎯 What Drives Me

I believe technology should serve humanity. I want to work on projects that truly make a difference in people's lives — whether it's a medical device or an intelligent industrial controller.
